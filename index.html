Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why should I join a club?

    It helps you make new friends who have similar interest. Helps you boost our talent and makes college life fun.

  • Which club should I join?

    You should choose a club of your own interest and personal liking. The choice for a club should also be made keeping in mind your skillset.

  • How to apply for a club?

    Just visit our website. Browse through different clubs available on the campus. Select the one you want to apply for. The tiny URL for registration is mentioned for each club. Just click those URL to apply for the club.

  • How to get the Club Explorer accreditation for my club?

    Just fill the form under the certification section on our website. Once you have submitted your application we will review it and get back to you with a plausible accreditataion for your club.

  • How to avail the experts suggestion?

    To get a personalised advise, send a mail to us on our official mail id mentioned on our website. Our experts will reach out to you as soon as possible. Alternatively you can give us a call on the mobile number mentioned on our website. You can also reach out to us through facebook, Instagram and Twitter.

  • Why can't I find all the clubs at one go?

    That's why we are here.
